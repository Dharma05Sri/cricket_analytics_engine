System Protocols (FAQ)

How does the 1,000-inning simulation work?
The AI utilizes a Monte Carlo simulation method. It extracts the strike rates, averages, and economy telemetry of the selected entities and runs a weighted probability algorithm 1,000 times to generate a win percentage.
Can I add missing players to the database?
Currently, the AI pulls from a locked `database.json` file. If a player is missing, you can submit a "Player Database Update Request" through the terminal on the left for future matrix integrations.
Are the player statistics updated live?
No. The telemetry is statically locked for educational and analytical simulation purposes. This ensures the simulation algorithms fire instantaneously without server-side latency.
How is the Kinetic Power Index calculated?
The Power Index is a synthesized rating out of 100. For batsmen, it heavily weights Strike Rate over Average. For bowlers, it cross-references total wickets against their economy containment parameters.
